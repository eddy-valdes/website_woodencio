Hi There

How do you do? My name is Woodencio Madeiros, a pretty normal name. It is my name after all.

I am a piece of wood. I have my body, my legs, my arms and my thoughts. I know I look a little stiff, but I am as open minded as a squared piece of wood could be. I also have glasses. I wear glasses because I have myopia and not because I want to make a fashion statement or something.

In my free time I like to wander around the garden enjoying life and beauty. I am very young but I have an old soul or something, because I like the slow pacing things. Maybe I am more older than I think or I inherit the experience of previous lives as a tree or something. I do not know for sure. What I know is that I like to enjoy the little things of life, because no one really knows what the future awaits for us.

During my travels around I make lots of friends and acquaintances that make life more bearable. Friendship is very important, is the family you choose and the one is going to be for you when you need it. You have to be for them too when they need it, of course. Friendship is a two way branch. Or is it a two way street? Any way, don’t take it from me, ask all my friends how awesome I am.
