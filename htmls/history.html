My History

Prepare the tissues, it's time for the tears. Well, not really. In reality my history is pretty boring stuff.

My story is fascinating. I was a piece of wood. Amazing, right? I was lying around in the floor collecting dust with other friendly pieces of wood and suddenly I came to life and decided that I want a website to share my persona so everyone in this planet and beyond knows me.

Some say that a powerful divine entity create everything around us, including us created in its same squared wooden image. Others say that millions of years ago there was a huge explosion called The Big Splinter that created the elementary particles that make up the universe and stars and galaxies were formed. And in a forgettable corner of the universe, on a random planet, a poorly evolved ape who had no idea what topic to cover for a final website project looked at the mess in his room and saw a piece of wood lying on the floor and he had an epiphany. Nonsenses.

So here I am living my life, thinking about the future no matter how depressing. I could end up working as a piece of furniture, like a chair, a coaster, a bookshelf or a coffee table. Or I could end up in an intense and short life burning as firewood in a barbecue, just like a famous actor consumed by the fame. Or I can end up in a boring life like the block that keeps the table from limping. We will see.

My family have a lot of history too. I came from a timber line of wooden members. My great-great-great-great-great-grandma was a pine tree stump, my great-great-grandpa was a wood mallet, my great-grandma was an hickory spoon, my mother was a war axe handle (we are a complex family) and I am an industrial serrated piece of pine wood with a lot of potential.

We also have enemies, specially my mother’s side of the family the trees. Axes, saws, machetes, in love teenagers with a knife carving a heart in a tree’s bark, chainsaws, termite, fire, humidity. But the worse of all are the humans, those idiots monkeys!
